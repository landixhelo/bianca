OUR HISTORY

Situated 6 km from Dajti Ekspres Cable Car, Bianca Restaurant&Hotel offers accommodation with a balcony. All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. Skanderbeg Square is 8 km from the villa, while Former Residence of Enver Hoxha is 9 km from the property.

Featuring river views, Bianca Hotel & Villa provides accommodation with patio, around 8.9 km from Dajti Ekspres Cable Car. There is a private entrance at the villa for the convenience of those who stay. There is a restaurant serving pizza cuisine, and free private parking is available.
